N-Body Simulator
==============

A simple N-body simulator animated with matplotlib. Just assign
the positions, velocities and masses and the simulation will take 
from there!

===
Examples included:
* earthMoon - The moon orbiting around the earth
* figureEight - A 3 body system that traces out a figure eight
* twelveBodies - A system of twelve bodies that has interesting behavior

===
Requirements:
* matplotlib
* ffmpeg
